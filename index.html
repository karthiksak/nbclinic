Understanding Homoeopathy

Homoeopathy is a system of alternative medicine created in 1796 by Dr. Samuel Hahnemann, based on the doctrine of "like cures like." A substance that causes symptoms in healthy individuals can cure similar symptoms in the sick. Remedies are prepared through homeopathic potentization — a process of serial dilution and vigorous shaking (succussion).
